Turning Dreams to Reality

After years of wishing I knew how to write code I have finally started to make it a reality!

For about a week now I have been using an app called "Mimo" that is full of simplified coding classes. It is a paid for app with $30 buying you a year subscription. To be honest, I haven't tried any other apps geared towards learning code but I really like what I've been able to do/learn so far. This is coming from someone who really only knows enough to get by on the computer. I learned a lot when I was a teenager but it's amazing how fast you can forget. I am only 27 years old as I am writing this post and with how fast technology advances; I tend to feel a little on the incompetent side when it comes to the newest platforms.
